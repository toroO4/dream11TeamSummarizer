Testing the updated AI analysis format with bold headings and Team 2 serving as the template for all teams.

Test Data

This test uses 3 sample teams to verify that Team 2's analysis structure serves as the template for all teams. The format includes:

  • Team Balance: Analysis of team composition
  • Captaincy Choice: Captain and vice-captain analysis
  • Match Advantage: Team's advantage for the match
  • Venue Strategy: How team suits the venue
  • Covariance Analysis: Player combinations and dependencies
  • Pitch Conditions: How team suits pitch conditions
  • Overall Rating: Comprehensive summary and recommendation
